## What players would know

This campaign uses the **D&D 2024 core rules** as the baseline.

If you’re excited about a character option that feels “weird,” bring it early—Qualihut supports customization, but it does so by making origins, institutions, and consequences matter in-world.

### Bard traditions (PC-facing)

- **Court / record / rhetoric bards:** use the bard options that support scholarship, rhetoric, and social leverage (default: **College of Lore**).
- **Tavern / road / dance bards:** use the bard options that support physical performance, rhythm, and crowd control (default: **College of Dance**).

### PC restrictions (current)

- **No Fey contact PCs** (Fey traditions exist in-world, but they’re NPC-facing; Fey-touched PC options are excluded).
- **No College of Valor PCs** (if you want a “marching-song” vibe, talk to the GM about a Lore/Dance reskin instead).
