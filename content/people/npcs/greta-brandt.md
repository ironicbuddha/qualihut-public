## What players would know

Greta Brandt owns The Brass Buckler, a City Watch watering hole. She looks like
someone who has heard every story twice and believes half of them. She runs a
clean bar in a dirty city and charges extra for anyone who tries to make that
her problem.

### Common rumors

- Greta can get a message to a captain faster than a runner can.
