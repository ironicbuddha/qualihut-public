## What players would know

![Toliver “Tolly” Brune](images/tolly-brune.png)

Toliver “Tolly” Brune is a street vendor who reliably appears wherever the [Royal Games](../../institutions/royal-games.md) gather crowds. He is aggressively friendly, addresses everyone as “my lord” (duke, stablehand, and street urchin alike), and sells a hot snack that is _almost_ a meal.

His stall sign reads:

> TOLLY'S HONEST MEALS
> SAUSAGE IN A BUN  
> (NAMED MEAT)

Tolly insists this is a mark of integrity. When pressed, he produces a small ledger and says, “Transparency, my lord. Every sausage recorded. First names only. Privacy laws.”

### At a glance

- Demeanor: too cheerful, too close, never embarrassed.
- Pitch: calls his product “fully licensed” and waves a stamp that is never quite right.
- Tell: he smiles like he has already won the argument, even when he is clearly losing.

### Common rumors

- If you eat his food before a big bet, your luck turns “honest” (for good or ill).
- Nobody has ever caught him lying; he only speaks in _legal shapes_ of truth.
