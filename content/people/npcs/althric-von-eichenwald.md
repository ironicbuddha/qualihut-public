## What players would know

![Lord Althric von Eichenwald](images/althric-von-eichenwald.png)
Lord Althric von Eichenwald is an elven noble of
[House von Eichenwald](../../institutions/house-von-eichenwald.md), associated
with imperial administration and “quiet oversight” work—the kind of authority
that arrives without fanfare and leaves you rearranging your life around an
appointment you didn’t request.

He tends to present as reasonable and restrained, which is exactly why people take him seriously.
