Mr. Duvalli is a wine merchant tied to [Vellum & Vine](../../locations/vellum-and-vine.md), a city-center shop known for tastings, private bottles, and clients who prefer not to be written down too clearly.

He is spoken of as connected to a vineyard outside the city, and as someone who can arrange fast movement of casks when normal couriers are too visible.

### Common rumors

- Duvalli’s best vintages are never on the menu; they are “for known names only.”
- His courier network is cleaner than most guild routes, but only on paper.
